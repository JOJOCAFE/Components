# 74HC02 DIP Pinout

Active-low pins are written with a leading slash, for example `/OE`.

| Manufacturer datasheet | DIP package checked |
|---|---|
| Texas Instruments SN74HC02: https://www.ti.com/lit/ds/symlink/sn74hc02.pdf | N, 14-pin PDIP |
