# 74HC164 DIP Pinout

Active-low pins are written with a leading slash, for example `/OE`.

|---|---|
| Texas Instruments SN74HC164: https://www.ti.com/lit/ds/symlink/sn74hc164.pdf | N, 14-pin PDIP |
